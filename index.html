Quick Setup Guide

Get up and running in minutes. All you need is an RTL-SDR dongle and a computer to act as the server.

1

Launch rtl_tcp

Run the server on your computer or Pi:

rtl_tcp -a 0.0.0.0 -p 1234
2

Connect App

Open CoronaSDR, enter your server's IP address and port.

3

Tune In

Adjust gain, pick a mode (like WFM for radio), and enjoy.

$ rtl_tcp -a 0.0.0.0
