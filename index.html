Local-first SDR listening

Clean SDR listening from your own rtl_tcp server.

CoronaSDR connects your iPhone to an RTL-SDR server on your LAN and gives you live spectrum, waterfall, multiple demod modes, and practical tuning controls without cloud dependency for radio data.

Quick setup

Compatible with `rtl_tcp` servers on Raspberry Pi, Linux, or macOS.

Built by Silviu YO6SAY

What you get

Live RF + DSP

AM, NFM, WFM, USB, LSB, and CW with squelch, AGC, and DSP filtering.

Spectrum + Waterfall

Real-time FFT and Metal-rendered waterfall with performance profiles.

Reliable audio path

Jitter-aware buffers, reconnect flow, and click-free retune transitions.

Station memory

Favorites, tags, and CSV/TSV import/export stored on device.

Advanced tuner controls

PPM, direct sampling, offset tuning, and bias-tee (hardware/server dependent).

Diagnostics

Throughput, buffer health, and performance traces for troubleshooting.

Quick setup

You need an RTL-SDR dongle and an `rtl_tcp` server reachable from your phone on the same LAN.

01

Start your SDR server

rtl_tcp -a 0.0.0.0 -p 1234

02

Connect from CoronaSDR

Enter your server host and port, then test connection.

03

Tune and listen

Pick mode, adjust gain and enjoy live spectrum + audio.
